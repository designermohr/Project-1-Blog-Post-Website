Juicy blueberry muffins

03.12.2021

This recipe for juicy blueberry muffins is very easy to prepare and always goes down well.

Ingredients for 12 servings

200 grams blueberries
230 grams flour
150 grams sugar
2 teaspons of baking soda
1 pinch salt
2 pcs eggs, room temperature
5 tablespoons of milk
100 grams butter, room temperature

Preparation steps

For the juicy blueberry muffins, first select the blueberries, wash them and drain them in a colander.

Preheat the oven to 180 ° C (top and bottom heat). Grease the hollows of the muffin tin with a little butter or line them with paper liners.

Then sift the flour into a bowl and mix with sugar, baking powder and a pinch of salt.

Your kids will love the muffins, but the blueberry stains will add extra work to your washing machine

Now stir the butter at room temperature with a hand mixer until creamy, then stir in the eggs and milk and stir well until foamy.

Then carefully stir in the flour mixture, process into a viscous dough and then fold in the blueberries.

Now fill the prepared muffin molds 2/3 full with the batter and bake in the preheated oven on the middle rack for 25 minutes. To check whether the muffins have been baked - do a stick test.

Then remove the blueberry muffins from the mold, let them cool on a wire rack and serve dusted with powdered sugar.

Tips on the recipe

The blueberries can also be replaced by currants, for example.

If you like it even more sophisticated, you can also add a colored meringue top to the blueberry muffins and decorate with pieces of chocolate, almond or nut - this is how perfect-tasting cupcakes are created.

All ingredients should be at room temperature when they are prepared.
